A Brief History


St.Alphonsa's Educational Society was founded in the year 1979, by a group of dedicated educators of Christian community and a few like-minded persons. Dr.Emmanuel eminent educationist being the chief founder, Mrs.Selvarani Ponnaiah was its first Chairman immediately succeeded by Sr. Diwaker Francis. The original society got divided into two and St.Alphonsa's Educational Development Society was formed. It is distinguished as a Christian Minority Society from the earlier Society.


The Society is an autonomous body and the School is Minority Christian Educational Intuition. Though, it is primarily for Christians, students irrespective of caste and creed are also admitted.


In administrative matters, the governing body's decision is final and binding. Though the School is recognized by the Government of Andhra Pradesh it is not receiving any financial aid either from the Government or any other source. Hence it is completely dependent on fees from students.
